INTRO TO FRONT END WEB DEVELOPMENT

This course assumes you have little or no experience in developing web applications. The classes will be held 3 nights a week, 3 hours/night (class and lab time) student will have a good understanding of the style languages of HTML and CSS as well as basic logic with JavaScript, and we will provide intros to some client side frameworks & libraries.

RUBY ON RAILS & POSTGRESQL

The students will spend 2 nights a week for 90 minutes/night. Each student will have a good understanding of the Ruby language, and Rails framework, and Relational databases with PostgreSQL through Rails Active Record.

**This course furthers the skills taught in the front end program and guides the learning through the full development lifecycle**
